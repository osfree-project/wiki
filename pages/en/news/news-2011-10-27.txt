==== 27 October, 2011 ====

A presentation of osFree on CC'2011

August 28, osFree team member Vadim Priluzkiy (aka Oxyd) has demonstrated 
osFree OS/2 personality working together with l4linux over L4 microkernel. 
The presentation was carried out during the Chaos Constructions'2011 yearly 
Computer Art Festival. (http://party11.cc.org.ru), the presentation video 
is here: http://youtube.com/watch?v=yxE6Jy3SQ04
